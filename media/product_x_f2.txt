Product X is a next-generation wireless headset designed for professional audio use.
It features noise-cancellation, a built-in microphone, and Bluetooth 5.3 technology.
The battery lasts up to 24 hours with continuous use.

To connect Product X:
1. Turn on the headset.
2. Press and hold the power button until the LED flashes blue.
3. Pair it with your device via Bluetooth settings.

If the headset does not connect, try resetting it by holding the power button for 10 seconds.
